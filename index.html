A Hidden Medieval Village Near San Gimignano

Just one hour from Florence and only 10 minutes from San Gimignano, Certaldo Alto is one of Tuscany’s most authentic hill towns — untouched by mass tourism and surrounded by rolling countryside, medieval architecture and timeless Italian atmosphere.

Visiting San Gimignano?

Most Tuscany day trips from Florence include San Gimignano for its famous medieval towers and historic center. But just a few minutes away, Certaldo Alto offers a quieter and more authentic Tuscan experience with fewer crowds, local restaurants, panoramic views and hidden photography spots.
